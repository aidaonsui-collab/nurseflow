The Problem

EHR Documentation Burden

Nurses spend up to 40% of their shift on charting. Poor EHR usability and excessive clicks lead to burnout and errors.

Scheduling Inefficiencies

Manual scheduling causes shift conflicts, overtime issues, and doesn't account for nurse preferences or work-life balance.

Handoff Errors

67% of handoff sheets contain errors - missing allergies, wrong medication info, risking patient safety.

Medication Administration

Interruptions during med prep cause errors. Lack of systematic feedback loops prevent continuous improvement.

Training Gaps

76% of nurses feel inadequately prepared to use their EHR systems, leading to frustration and potential errors.

No Feedback Systems

Lack of real-time feedback on documentation errors prevents nurses from improving their workflow.

Our Solution Approach

AI Documentation Assistant

Voice-to-note technology that listens to nurse-patient interactions and auto-generates documentation. Save 2+ hours per shift.

Smart Handoff Tool

Mobile-first standardized handoff system with built-in error checking. Reduce handoff errors by 80%.

Intelligent Scheduling

AI-powered scheduling that balances nurse preferences, hospital needs, and work-life balance. Reduce overtime costs by 30%.

Medication Safety Check

Workflow optimization and distraction-free zones. Real-time verification to prevent administration errors.
